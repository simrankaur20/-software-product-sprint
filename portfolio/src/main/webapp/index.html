I Received Certificate of “Special Appreciation of Efforts” from HelpAge India for creating awareness and assisting in raising funds for the care of elderly. I believe in using my skills to help the community. I had made a chat bot called “Companion Bot” for Dementia patients. Dementia patients often forget their basic information and due to this disease, they lack social interaction in their lives. The badly need a friend so that they can communicate without any hesitation. The bot is a companion for dementia patients. The patient can ask for his basic information from the bot again and again, talk to the bot about his day, play songs, watch movies by links provided with the bot, etc. So this was a project implemented to help dementia patients.
